    SA Top 5 Rappers




  • A-Reece



    Born Lehlogonolo Ronald Mataboge, A-Reece is one of SA rap game’s most-watched youngin'. He is one of the most prominent artists within The Wrecking Crew stable, which was formed after he and fellow stable-mates Benchmarq left Ambitiouz Entertainment. His alias is inspired by his star sign Aries, the first astrological sign in the Zodiac often closely associated with Greek mythology of the ram. So the ‘A-R’ stands for ‘Above Reality’, which means that I’m a dreamer, because I know that dreams can come true. The ‘ee’ stands for ‘Exceptionally Emotional’. As a human you are emotional, sometimes you are extremely happy or sad or whatever.



  • Blxckie



    Blxckie is a 21-year-old rapper from Durban who started out as a rapper in Durban rap group Clout Internet Boys. He found himself stuck in Johannesburg during the 21-day lockdown in March 2020 and released plenty of music on SoundCloud where he celebrated 1 million streams on the app. He previously mentioned that he wanted his 12-track offering to serve as an introduction of his artistry to the music industry. On the project Blxckie features only a few big-name rappers, including Nasty C and Flvme.



  • Lucasrap$



    Rapper & Songwriter, Luke Duncan Malong. Born in Cape Town but spent most his life in the City of Durban. Lucas quickly adopted the musical culture at the very young age of 8. This early passion and love for music lead him to the entertainment industry. Lucasraps unlocked the door to his musical journey in October 2016; by performing alongside the iconic, Nasty C; at the East Coast Radio Hauwei Durban Day



  • Nasty C



    Nsikayesizwe David Junior Ngcobo, known professionally as Nasty C, is a South African rapper, singer, songwriter, and record producer. After releasing three mixtapes, one being "Undisputed" and two EP, Ngcobo released his debut album Bad Hair, and Strings and Bling which was associated and supported by his first tour, The Ivyson Tour.



  • Priddy Ugly



    Priddy Ugly whose real name is Ricardo Moloi is a South African rapper born in Luanda, Angola on 2nd April 1992. He was, however, raised in Meadowlands, and later in Kempton Park, South Africa. He has always been an achiever from the beginning even during his teenage years when he was active in athletics.
