    Nsikayesizwe David Junior Ngcobo, known professionally as Nasty C, is a South African rapper, singer, songwriter, and record producer. After releasing three mixtapes, one being "Undisputed" and two EP, Ngcobo released his debut album Bad Hair, and Strings and Bling which was associated and supported by his first tour, The Ivyson Tour.
